FAQ's

  • What is self storage?
  • Self storage is shorthand for ‘self-service storage’. It can mean any space – such as a room, locker or unit – that you can rent on a short or long-term basis to securely store your possessions in.
  • How do I pay for my storage?
  • We offer a number of payment options – you can spread the cost with a monthly Direct Debit, or make payments using your credit card, debit card or bank transfer.
  • What are the opening hours?

  • Our reception opening hours are:

    • 08.30-18.00 Monday to Friday
    • 08.30-17.00 Saturday
    • 10.00-16.00 Sunday

    We offer free 24 hour access to your storage unit. page for more information.

  • Is there parking on site?
  • Yes. We offer on site parking spaces for all customers.
  • Does the facility have trolleys available?
  • Yes. We have trollies available at our facility. We also provide pallet trucks and forklifting services.
  • Does the facility have lifts?
  • Yes. Our facility has two lifts. If you have any mobility issues, please ask a member of staff about ground floor units.
  • How secure is my unit?
  • Our premises are described as being of a fortress build with all access fortified. Units are made from steel and we have fire and theft precautions in place for added protection. You have responsibility to ensure you keep your unit locked. CCTV is also in operation on site, as well as fob activated secure access and the premises have a monitored security alarm. We have full control of our perimeter fence and building as we do not share our premises with another company.
