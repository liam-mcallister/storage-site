Business Storage

At The Storage Nook, we provide a wide range of secure self storage units for business customers. Our promise to always be the cheapest means you’ll always get a great rate, and our flexible approach means that you can store with us for as little as one week. You can also change your storage unit size whenever you need to. Give us a call and talk to our friendly experts to find the right storage solution for you.

We have a fully accessible building with ground floor level-access and 1st floor with stairs & lift access. We have toilets & refreshments on site as well as free parking, free use of trolleys and free wifi access.

Pop In, Anytime

You can access your unit at any time, day or night, 7 days a week using the FOB provided upon sign up.

Office Opening Hours:

  • Mon to Fri 8.30am to 6pm
  • Sat 9.00am to 4pm

With a minimum term from just one week, you can stay as long as you like. Don't feel under pressure to commit to long term contracts. Self storage is about flexibility for you, to store as you need, for the time period you require.

Don't pay for more space than you need. We will put you into the smallest unit possible to fit your belongings, that way you can be sure you are not overpaying for your requirement.

If you find you need more space than you started with, just say the word and we’ll upgrade you to a bigger unit. Similarly, if you want to reduce the size of your storage unit (and your monthly bill) we’re ready to accommodate you, in person, online or at the end of the phone.

Security

Here at The Storage Nook we take security seriously. Your belongings are securely stored at all times in a fortified premises monitored by CCTV, intruder alarm, fire detection systems.

We have staff on site at our facility, seven days a week during office opening hours. As well as helping to make your life easier, they're fully trained to spot – and react to – any suspicious behaviour or activity identified, creating another layer of safety.
